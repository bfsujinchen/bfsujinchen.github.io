This web is a basic introduction to Trados.

This web is intended to make some brief introduction to Trados for translators and people who want to be a translator in the future.

As a useful CAT tool, Trados is beneficial for individuals who are interest in translation and want to be translators in the future.

Gone are the days of opening two MS Word documents side-by-side to manually translate a document.

Over 270,000 translators and 80% of the localization community use Trados.
